Fascinating Facts about Washington State

Here are some interesting facts about Washington State:

  • Washington is home to the famous Mount Rainier, an active stratovolcano and the highest mountain in the state.
  • The state's official flower is the Coast Rhododendron.
  • Seattle, often referred to as the "Emerald City," is known for its rainy climate and vibrant music scene.
