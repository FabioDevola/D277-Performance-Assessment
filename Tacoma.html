Tacoma is a city located in the Puget Sound region of Washington State.

  • Classification: Urban
  • Tacoma average income: $66,758 vs. Washington average income : $78,687

About Tacoma

Tacoma boasts a mix of industrial heritage, stunning waterfront views, and a thriving arts and cultural scene. The city is known for its historic architecture and diverse neighborhoods.
