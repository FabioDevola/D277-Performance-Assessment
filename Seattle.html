Explore Seattle

Seattle is the largest city and the capital of Washington State.

  • Population: Approximately 750,000
  • Year Incorporated: 1869
  • Region: Puget Sound
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income Level: Above State Average

About Seattle

Seattle is a vibrant and diverse city located in the heart of Washington State. Known for its iconic Space Needle and stunning waterfront, Seattle offers a mix of cultural experiences, tech innovation, and natural beauty.
